Connecting volunteers with communities

HelpOut is a platform created to connect motivated volunteers with volunteer organizations that need help.

What is HelpOut?

HelpOut is a web platform that allows volunteers to easily find volunteering opportunities and allows volunteer organizations to find people who want to help.

Whether you want to help your local community, participate in environmental actions, or gain experience through volunteering, HelpOut brings people together for a better cause.
